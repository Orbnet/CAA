Few Words About Us

Americans Against Consumerism or AAC for short, is a small movement based around the idea that real progress towards the betterment of our society starts with a change in culture. That means it starts with you, the individual, and by following the three step action plan laid out below then maybe change can happen.

Work

Like all goals, effort needs to be put in. Whenever you have the chance put in the time to remove excess items, by giving them to the less fortunate or by recycling them.

Inform

It's not just you in this fight, there are others too, even if they don't know it yet. So it's up to you to inform them by spreading the message on social media.

Learn

Educate yourself beyond the information provided here, this is a multi-faceted issue with wide ranging topics and concerns to learn about.

Call To Action

Follow the three steps to changing our community: Work towards reducing your own consumption and giving what you don't want or need to those around you, inform others about these ideas and your beliefs, and educate yourself further.
